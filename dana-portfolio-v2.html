About

The best products begin with people.

I'm a Business and Computer Science student at Minerva University, and I've lived and studied across four countries. That taught me great products aren't one size fits all. They're shaped by the people who use them.

I pair that perspective with technical and analytical skills to solve real problems: a civic education game with the San Francisco Department of Public Health, robotics product concepts at Skild AI, and Sony's platform adapted for users across countries.

I'm endlessly curious about how AI can serve people, and I care as much about the teams and communities behind a product as the product itself. I've built both from scratch.
